Welcome to Stroke Services Foundation

Our mission is to provide comprehensive care and support for stroke patients and their families. We are dedicated to improving the quality of life for those affected by stroke through education, advocacy, and research.

  • Rehabilitation Services
  • Support Groups
  • Educational Workshops
  • Research and Advocacy
